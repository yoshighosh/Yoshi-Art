Charcoal Representations of Science Fiction Characters

This is one of my earliest science fiction drawings depicting my favorite guardians of the galaxy character "Baby Groot"

After Drawing my Depicition of "Baby Groot" I decide to go along with my science fiction theme and created a drawing of "Star Lord" or otherwise known as Peter Quill.

This was inspired by one of my favorite books The Lost Hero. This depicts a bronze dragon name Fetus.
